How to make homemade mustard

Original Recipe

Step 1

Add water to a non-reactive saucepan with the mustard powder, salt, turmeric, garlic purée or powder, and paprika. Whisk the mixture until combined.

Step 2

Cook over medium-low heat until the mixture cooks down to a thick paste.

Should be pasty and not watery

Make sure it's medium-low heat!

Step 3

Whisk the vinegar into the mustard paste, and cook until the mixture reaches the desired consistency.

Mix throughoughly

Last step:

Enjoy!
